Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

Authentication Data

When you sign in through Unity Authentication, we collect unique identifiers and authentication tokens.

Leaderboard Data

We collect and store your scores and related performance data for the purpose of displaying leaderboards.

How We Use Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and allow access to certain features of the game.
  • Leaderboards: To display user scores and rankings within the game, enhancing your competitive experience.
  • Improving Services: We analyze the collected data to improve game features and your user experience.

Sharing of Information

Third-Party Services: We use Unity services to manage authentication and leaderboards. Unity's privacy policy can be found here.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We take measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

User Rights

Access and Control: You can access, update, or delete your data by contacting us.

Consent and Withdrawal: You can withdraw your consent for data collection and processing at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the policy on our website.
